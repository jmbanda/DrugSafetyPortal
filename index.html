APIs Used

Anybody in the pharmaceutical industry knows that drugs are a messy thing to deal with... from the a data perspective. Here are all the tools needed to bring this project to life

NCBO BioPortal

For text annotation into ontology concepts

NLM MESH on demand

For drug name linkage with Medline and Pubmed

NLM Medline

For literature querying of adverse events

NLM Pubmed

For literature relevant retrieval

openFDA

For FAERS reporting data gathering

bio2rdf.org

Extrenal linkage to over 150 datasets via MeSH identifiers

Data Sources Used

For the drug-drug interaction data we have scraped predictions generated from research publications and from these sources (and others not listed here)

  • Stanford University

    * Iyer, S. V., Harpaz, R., LePendu, P., Bauer-Mehren, A., & Shah, N. H. (2014). Mining clinical text for signals of adverse drug-drug interactions. Journal of the American Medical Informatics Association : JAMIA, 21(2), 353–362. http://doi.org/10.1136/amiajnl-2013-001612

    * J.M. Banda, A. Callahan, R. Winnenburg, H. Strasberg, A. Cami, B. R eis, S. Vilar, G. Hripcsak, M. Dumontier, N.H. Shah. “Feasibility of prioritizing Drug-Drug-Event Associations Found in Electronic Health Records” accepted Drug Safety. 2015.

  • Harvard University

    * Cami, A., Manzi, S., Arnold, A., & Reis, B. Y. (2013). Pharmacointeraction Network Models Predict Unknown Drug-Drug Interactions. PLoS ONE, 8(4), e61468. http://doi.org/10.1371/journal.pone.0061468

  • Columbia University

    * Vilar, S., Uriarte, E., Santana, L., Lorberbaum, T., Hripcsak, G., Friedman, C., & Tatonetti, N. P. (2014). Similarity-based modeling in large-scale prediction of drug-drug interactions. Nature Protocols, 9(9), 2147–2163. http://doi.org/10.1038/nprot.2014.151

    * Tatonetti, N. P., Fernald, G. H., & Altman, R. B. (2012). A novel signal detection algorithm for identifying hidden drug-drug interactions in adverse event reports. Journal of the American Medical Informatics Association : JAMIA, 19(1), 79–85. http://doi.org/10.1136/amiajnl-2011-000214
